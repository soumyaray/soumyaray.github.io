Software Packages

SEMinR

This R package offers a natural feeling, domain-specific language to build and estimate structural equation models in R. It uses variance-based PLS estimation to model both composite and common-factor constructs. It offers High-level functions to quickly specify interactions and complicated structural models. And it offers high performance, multi-core bootstrapping.
